What is the ideal travel season for tropical destinations?

Tropical destinations are best visited during the dry season, usually from November to April, to avoid heavy rainfall.

How accurate are the budget estimates?

Budget estimates are based on average costs for accommodation, meals, and activities in each region. For the most accurate estimate, check current pricing on travel websites.

How often is the weather data updated?

The weather data can be updated every 24 hours using cron jobs or serverless functions to ensure accuracy. This allows the recommendations to remain up-to-date with current conditions.
